About games Offline Progress

Here you can see the code for the offline progress in the game made by me. I think it is a really nice feature in the game even though not a necessary one. I remember fighting with it for quite a long time until i finally got it to work.
The mechanic basically starts counting time after the user has left the game and remembers it until the player returns. While the player is not playing the player still gets progress towards their relative material gains.
